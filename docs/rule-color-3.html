Rule: Color contrast of text: Enhanced

Rule ID Color 3
Conformance Recommended
Definition Text content must exceed Color Contrast Ratio (CCR) of 4.5 for large and/or bolded text and 7 for any other size or style of text.
Purpose
  • The higher the color contrast of text the more easy it is to read, especially for people with visual impairments.
Conformance Recommended
WCAG Success Criteria

Success Criterion 1.4.6 Contrast (Enhanced)

Other Related Success Criterion:

  • Success Criterion 1.4.1 Use of Color
  • Success Criterion 1.4.3 Contrast (Minimum)
Rule Category Color/Content
Rule Scope Element
Techniques
  • Change the foreground color to a more complementary color to the background color.
  • Change the background color to a more complementary color to the foreground color.
  • Remove background images or verify they do not compromise color contrast requirements.
Information Links
  • WCAG 2.0 Success Criterion 1.4.6 Contrast (Enhanced): The visual presentation of text and images of text has a contrast ratio of at least 7:1, with exceptions
  • How to meet Success Criterion 1.4.6 Contrast (Minimum): The visual presentation of text and images of text has a contrast ratio of at least 7:1, with exceptions
  • G17: Ensuring that a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 exists between text (and images of text) and background behind the text
  • G18: Ensuring that a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 exists between text (and images of text) and background behind the text
  • G148: Not specifying background color, not specifying text color, and not using technology features that change those default
  • G174: Providing a control with a sufficient contrast ratio that allows users to switch to a presentation that uses sufficient contrast
